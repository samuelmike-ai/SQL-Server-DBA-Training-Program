## Assessment Criteria

### Career Development and Planning (25%)
- **Excellent (A):** Creates comprehensive career development strategy with detailed planning and advancement
- **Good (B):** Develops solid career planning with good development frameworks
- **Satisfactory (C):** Basic career development with minimal strategic planning
- **Needs Improvement (D/F):** Inadequate career development and planning

### Leadership and Team Management (25%)
- **Excellent (A):** Demonstrates advanced leadership and team management capabilities
- **Good (B):** Shows solid leadership and team management skills
- **Satisfactory (C):** Basic leadership with limited team management features
- **Needs Improvement (D/F):** Poor leadership and team management understanding

### Communication and Documentation (25%)
- **Excellent (A):** Builds sophisticated communication and documentation systems
- **Good (B):** Creates functional communication with good documentation
- **Satisfactory (C):** Basic communication with limited documentation features
- **Needs Improvement (D/F):** Minimal communication and documentation implementation

### Business Acumen and Strategic Thinking (25%)
- **Excellent (A):** Implements advanced business strategy and strategic thinking
- **Good (B):** Shows good business acumen and strategic capabilities
- **Satisfactory (C):** Basic business understanding with limited strategic thinking
- **Needs Improvement (D/F):** Poor business acumen and strategic thinking
